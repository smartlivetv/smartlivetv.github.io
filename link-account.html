How can I Link Your Amazon Account to Your TV Using Amazon.com/mytv?


Linking your Amazon account to your TV using amazon.com/mytv is a quick and secure way to access Prime Video content on your big screen. Whether you're using a Smart TV, streaming stick, or game console, the process involves launching the Prime Video app, retrieving a unique registration code, and entering it on the Amazon website. This ensures your device is authorized and ready for seamless streaming. Follow the steps below to complete the setup and enjoy your favorite shows and movies in minutes.

How to Link Your Amazon Account to Your TV Using amazon.com/mytv?

  1. Turn on your Smart TV or streaming device and ensure it’s connected to the internet.
  2. Open the Amazon Prime Video app from your device’s app menu.
  3. Select "Sign In" or "Register on the Amazon Website".
  4. A unique registration code (MyTV code) will appear on your screen.
  5. On a separate device (phone, tablet, or computer), go to amazon.com/mytv.
  6. Sign in with your Amazon account credentials.
  7. Enter the registration code exactly as shown on your TV screen.
  8. Once accepted, your device will be linked and ready to stream Prime Video content.
