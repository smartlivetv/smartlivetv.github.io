How to activate mytv on device using amazon.com/mytv?


Activating your device through amazon.com/mytv is quick and easy. Whether you're using a smart TV, streaming stick, or game console, this secure link helps you connect your Amazon account to your device seamlessly. By entering the activation code shown on your screen into the website, you can start enjoying Amazon Prime Video in just minutes. Make sure your device is internet-connected and you’re signed into the correct Amazon account for a smooth experience.

How to Activate Your Device Using amazon.com/mytv

  • Turn on your smart TV or streaming device and open the Prime Video app.
  • Note the 6- or 7-character activation code displayed on your screen.
  • On a separate device, go to amazon.com/mytv in a web browser.
  • Sign in to your Amazon account using your login credentials.
  • Enter the activation code in the field provided on the page.
  • Click “Register Device” and wait for confirmation on your TV screen.
  • Your device is now linked and ready to stream Prime Video.
