How do I Register my Device on Amazon MyTv?


Registering your device on Amazon MyTV lets you stream Prime Video content on your smart TV or streaming device in just a few steps. Start by opening the Prime Video app on your TV, where you’ll see a unique registration code displayed. Next, use your phone, tablet, or computer to visit amazon.com/mytv, sign in with your Amazon account, and enter the code. Once submitted, your device will be instantly linked, giving you access to thousands of movies and shows on Prime Video.

How to Register Your Device on Amazon MyTV (amazon.com/mytv)

  1. Open the Amazon Prime Video app on your smart TV or streaming device.
    If the app is not installed, download it from your device’s app store.
  2. Select “Sign In” or “Register on the Amazon Website” to display a unique registration code on your TV screen.
    Note or keep this code visible; you’ll need it for the next steps.
  3. On your phone, tablet, or computer, open a web browser and go to amazon.com/mytv.
    You can use any device with internet access and a web browser.
  4. Sign in with your Amazon account credentials if prompted.
    If you don’t have an account, create one following the on-screen instructions.
  5. Enter the registration code from your TV screen into the field provided on the website.
  6. Click “Register Device” or “Submit”.
  7. Wait for confirmation—your TV or device will automatically update and grant you access to Prime Video content.
    If you encounter issues, ensure your device is connected to the internet and the code is entered exactly as shown (case-insensitive).

Once registered, your device will be linked to your Amazon account and ready to stream Prime Video.
You can manage or deregister devices anytime from your Amazon account settings.
Enjoy watching your favorite movies and TV shows!
