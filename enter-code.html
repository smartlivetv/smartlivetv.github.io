How do I enter a code on Amazon MyTV?


Activating Prime Video on your smart TV or streaming device is quick and secure with the Amazon MyTV code. Simply open the Prime Video app on your device, select "Register on the Amazon Website," and note the code shown on your screen. Next, go to amazon.com/mytv on your phone or computer, sign in with your Amazon account, and enter the code. Within moments, your device will be linked and ready to stream your favorite Prime Video content seamlessly.

How to Enter a Code on Amazon MyTV (amazon.com/mytv)

  • Launch the Amazon Prime Video app on your smart TV, streaming device, or console.
  • On your TV screen, select “Sign in and start watching” or “Register on the Amazon Website” to display a unique activation code.
  • On a separate device (phone, tablet, or computer), open a web browser and go to amazon.com/mytv.
  • Sign in with your Amazon account credentials, if prompted.
  • Enter the activation code from your TV screen into the field provided on the website.
  • Click the “Register Device” or “Submit” button.
  • Wait for confirmation—your device will refresh and you’ll have access to Prime Video content.

This process works for smart TVs, Amazon Fire TV, Roku, Apple TV, PlayStation, Xbox, and other supported devices.
If you encounter any issues, double-check your internet connection and ensure the code is entered correctly.
Enjoy streaming your favorite shows and movies on Prime Video!
