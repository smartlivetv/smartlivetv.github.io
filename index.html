How to Set Up and Activate Your Device at Amazon.com/mytv


Easily connect your smart TV, streaming stick, or media device to your Amazon account using amazon.com/mytv. With just one activation code, you can link your device and start enjoying Prime Video’s vast library of movies, series, and exclusive Amazon Originals in minutes.

What Is the Amazon MyTV Activation Code?

When you open the Prime Video app on your device, a unique 6- or 7-character activation code will appear on your screen. This code is used to securely link your device to your Amazon account. Each code is unique and time-sensitive, so it must be entered correctly and promptly at amazon.com/mytv to complete the setup.

How to Activate Your Device Using Amazon.com/mytv

Follow these simple steps to register your device:

  • Launch Prime Video on your smart TV, streaming stick, game console, or other compatible device.
  • Note the Activation Code displayed on the screen.
  • On a separate device (phone, tablet, or computer), go to amazon.com/mytv.
  • Sign in to your Amazon account.
  • Enter the Activation Code in the provided field and click Register Device.
  • Your device will automatically refresh and be ready to stream.

Compatible Devices

You can activate the following devices using Amazon.com/mytv:

  • Smart TVs (Samsung, LG, Sony, Hisense, etc.)
  • Amazon Fire TV & Fire Stick
  • Roku
  • Apple TV
  • PlayStation & Xbox
  • Blu-ray players with Prime Video app

Troubleshooting Tips

  • Code expired? Restart the app to generate a new code.
  • Wrong account? Sign out and log in with the correct Amazon account.
  • Can’t see the code? Check your internet connection and restart your device.
